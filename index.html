Welcome to the technical interview

Instructions

The interview is divided into two major parts:

Part 1: Solving of simple algorithms
An example of how to solve an algorithm will first be given. This will be followed by a few more algorithms with decreasing amount of instructions.

Part 2: Implementation of a simple game
The interviewer will first explain how the game works followed by a walkthrough through the codebase and then a number of simple functions will need to be implemented

Tips:
  • Talk. Explain to the interviewer what you are doing
  • Ask questions.
  • Google is your friend
